A.P.J Abdul kalam

1931 - 2015

Missile Man Of India

In the heart of India, there dwelled a man of dreams, A visionary, a scientist, by many esteemed. With eyes that mirrored the stars, and a heart pure and grand, He became the Missile Man, the nation's guiding hand. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, his name etched in gold, A story of resilience, of brilliance, we're told. Born in a humble abode, with dreams in his eyes, He reached for the galaxies, touching the skies. In the realm of science, he found his sacred quest, To harness the atom, to become the very best. Aghast by the horrors of destructive force, He sought peaceful uses, a different course. With Agni, Prithvi, Trishul, and more, He made India's defense truly galore. But his missiles were not just steel and fire, They were the wings of hope, reaching higher and higher. He soared like a phoenix, from Rameswaram's shore, To Rashtrapati Bhavan, the nation to adore. Yet, in his rise, he remained grounded and true, A people's president, for me and for you. Through his words and wisdom, he kindled the spark, In every young heart, in light and in dark. He taught us to dream, to never give in, To conquer the world, and let aspirations begin. The Missile Man of India, a true guiding light, His legacy shines on, both day and night. In our hearts, in our dreams, he'll forever reside, Dr. Kalam, our hero, our nation's pride.

  • People's President, A Gracious Heart: Rashtrapati Bhavan's doors flung wide, A people's president, humility as his guide. With a heart so warm, and wisdom profound, He won every heart, the nation's love unbound.
  • Teacher of Dreams:In classrooms, he sowed the seeds of desire, Encouraging young minds to aim ever higher. Teaching not just science but values that last, For him, every student was a dream to be cast.
  • A Legacy Eternal:Though he left us, his spirit's alive, In the dreams of young India, he continues to thrive. An icon of inspiration, a radiant star, Dr. Kalam's legacy shines near and far.
